# Color Scheme Update - Official WISAL Branding

## New Color Palette (From Official Logo)

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Green**: `#2D7A3E` (WISAL Green) - Replaces `#1F3B66` (Blue)
- **Primary Dark**: `#1F5A2E` (Dark Green) - Replaces `#2a4d7a` (Dark Blue)
- **Primary Light**: `#4CAF50` (Light Green)

### Accent Colors
- **Accent Red**: `#C73E3A` (WISAL Red) - Replaces `#FFC300` (Yellow)
- **Accent Dark**: `#A52A26` (Dark Red) - Replaces `#FFD700` (Light Yellow)
- **Accent Light**: `#E57373` (Light Red)

### Brand Colors
Extracted directly from the official WISAL logo gradient:
- Green represents growth, trust, and Saudi heritage
- Red represents energy, action, and warmth
- Gradient symbolizes the buyer journey from browsing to purchasing

## Design Philosophy

The new color scheme is based on the official WISAL logo:
1. **Green (#2D7A3E)** - Growth, trust, Saudi heritage, natural connection
2. **Red (#C73E3A)** - Energy, action, warmth, Saudi national colors
3. **Gradient Effect** - Represents the journey from browsing (calm) to purchasing (action)

This authentic branding creates a cohesive identity that honors the WISAL name (وصال - "connection") and reflects Saudi culture while maintaining modern e-commerce standards.
