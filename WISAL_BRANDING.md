# WISAL Brand Identity

## Official Logo
The WISAL logo features elegant Arabic calligraphy (وصال) with a distinctive green-to-red gradient, symbolizing connection, growth, and Saudi heritage.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
Based on the official WISAL logo gradient:

- **Primary Green**: `#2D7A3E` - Main brand color (from logo top)
- **Primary Dark**: `#1F5A2E` - Darker shade for depth
- **Primary Light**: `#4CAF50` - Lighter shade for highlights

### Accent Colors
- **Accent Red**: `#C73E3A` - From logo gradient (bottom)
- **Accent Dark**: `#A52A26` - Darker red for hover states
- **Accent Light**: `#E57373` - Lighter red for subtle accents

### Neutral Colors
- **Text Dark**: `#2C2C2C` - Primary text
- **Text Gray**: `#5A5A5A` - Secondary text
- **Background**: `#FFFFFF` - Clean white background

## Brand Meaning

### وصال (WISAL)
The Arabic word "وصال" (Wisal) means "connection" or "union" - perfectly representing an e-commerce marketplace that connects buyers and sellers across Saudi Arabia.

### Color Symbolism

**Green (#2D7A3E)**
- Growth and prosperity
- Trust and reliability
- Connection to Saudi heritage
- Natural and organic feel

**Red (#C73E3A)**
- Energy and passion
- Urgency and action (deals, sales)
- Saudi national colors
- Warmth and hospitality

**Gradient Effect**
- The green-to-red gradient represents the journey from browsing (green/calm) to purchasing (red/action)
- Symbolizes the connection between traditional Saudi values (green) and modern commerce (red)
- Creates visual interest and premium feel

## Usage Guidelines

### Header & Navigation
- Use gradient background: `from-[#2D7A3E] to-[#1F5A2E]`
- Hover states use accent red: `#C73E3A`
- Cart badge and notifications: Red accent

### Buttons & CTAs
- Primary actions: Red accent `#C73E3A`
- Secondary actions: Green `#2D7A3E`
- Hover states: Darker variants

### Links & Interactive Elements
- Default: Green `#2D7A3E`
- Hover: Red `#C73E3A`
- Active: Dark red `#A52A26`

### Backgrounds
- Hero sections: Green gradient
- Feature highlights: Light red tints
- Cards: White with subtle shadows

## Typography
- Arabic: Clean, modern Arabic fonts
- English: Sans-serif for clarity
- Hierarchy: Bold for headings, regular for body

## Accessibility
- All color combinations meet WCAG AA standards
- Sufficient contrast ratios for readability
- Color is not the only indicator of state

## Brand Voice
- Welcoming and trustworthy
- Modern yet respectful of tradition
- Professional but approachable
- Saudi-first, globally aware
